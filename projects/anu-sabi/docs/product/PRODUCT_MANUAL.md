## What this manual is

Anu-Sabi (UI brand: **SabiMo?**) is a mobile word game built around a single idea: **read nonsense aloud until a real phrase clicks**. This manual explains what that means for players — why the game exists, how it feels, and where it is headed.

## One-paragraph summary

**SabiMo?** asks players to decode phonetic gibberish into real Filipino and world phrases — often by **reading the gibberish aloud** until the answer surfaces. Sessions are short, difficulty scales with timers and streak multipliers, and progress lives on the device (coins, badges, daily streak). The product is shipping **offline-first** (Phase 1), with **online services** (Phase 2) and **Bluetooth party play** (Phase 3) planned for later.
