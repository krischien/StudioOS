# 1. Purpose

This document defines the complete economy and player progression systems of Anu Sabi.

The economy is not limited to coins or purchases. It encompasses every reward, unlock, progression path, collectible, and long-term motivation that encourages players to return.

The guiding principle is simple:

> **Every session should feel rewarding, regardless of who wins.**

---

# 2. Economy Philosophy

The economy should:

* Reward participation, not only victory.
* Encourage creativity over competition.
* Make progression visible.
* Avoid "pay-to-win" mechanics.
* Promote long-term engagement.
* Give players meaningful goals beyond winning matches.

Players should always leave a game with something gained.

---

# 3. Design Goals

Our economy should:

* Encourage daily play.
* Reward social interaction.
* Celebrate creativity.
* Support future content expansions.
* Remain balanced over years of updates.
* Be easy for new players to understand.

---

# 4. Progression Loop

```text
Play Match
    ↓
Earn XP & Coins
    ↓
Complete Missions
    ↓
Unlock Rewards
    ↓
Customize Profile
    ↓
Unlock Content Packs
    ↓
Play Again
```

Progression should reinforce gameplay rather than distract from it.

---

# 5. Player XP

XP represents overall player experience.

Players earn XP by:

* Completing matches
* Finishing rounds
* Receiving votes
* Winning rounds
* Completing daily missions
* Completing weekly challenges
* Participating in events
* Trying new game modes

Example XP Rewards

| Action           |  XP |
| ---------------- | --: |
| Finish Match     | 100 |
| Round Win        |  25 |
| Match Victory    |  75 |
| Daily Mission    | 150 |
| Weekly Challenge | 500 |

Values are placeholders and will be balanced during testing.

---

# 6. Player Levels

Levels represent long-term progression.

Benefits of leveling up may include:

* New avatars
* Titles
* Borders
* Emotes
* Profile badges
* Content pack unlocks
* Cosmetic rewards

Levels should not increase gameplay power.

---

# 7. Coins

Coins are the primary in-game currency.

Coins can be earned through:

* Playing matches
* Winning matches
* Daily rewards
* Weekly challenges
* Seasonal events
* Achievements

Coins can be spent on:

* Cosmetic items
* Avatars
* Profile decorations
* Content packs
* Event items

Coins cannot purchase competitive advantages.

---

# 8. Premium Currency (Future)

A premium currency may be introduced after launch.

Potential uses:

* Premium cosmetics
* Exclusive profile themes
* Limited-time bundles
* Seasonal passes

Premium currency must never create unfair gameplay advantages.

# 12. Daily Rewards

Players receive rewards for consecutive logins.

Example

| Day | Reward         |
| --- | -------------- |
| 1   | Coins          |
| 2   | XP Boost       |
| 3   | Avatar Sticker |
| 4   | Coins          |
| 5   | Content Token  |
| 6   | Profile Frame  |
| 7   | Mystery Reward |

Missing a day should not completely reset progress. A forgiving streak system is preferred.

---

# 13. Daily Missions

Examples

* Play 2 Matches
* Vote in Every Round
* Win 1 Match
* Try a New Category
* Invite a Friend

Daily missions should take 10–20 minutes to complete.

# 14. Weekly Challenges

# 19. Economy Balancing

The economy should avoid:

* Excessive grinding
* Pay-to-win systems
* Reward inflation
* Currency overload
* Overly complex progression

Regular balancing updates should be expected after launch.

---

# 20. Anti-Inflation Strategy

To maintain a healthy economy:

* Introduce new cosmetic sinks.
* Rotate featured items.
* Keep premium items optional.
* Balance reward rates regularly.
* Monitor average player earnings.

# 22. Success Metrics

The economy succeeds when:

* Players return daily.
* Rewards feel satisfying.
* Progress is visible.
* Cosmetics feel desirable.
* Spending remains optional.
* Players stay engaged for months or years.
