# 💎 Monetization Strategy

> *Players should spend money because they love the game, not because they feel forced to.*

---

# Purpose

The purpose of monetization is to sustain the continued development of Anu Sabi while maintaining a fair, enjoyable, and trustworthy experience for every player.

Monetization should enhance the product without creating unfair advantages.

---

# Monetization Philosophy

Every monetization decision should follow these principles:

- Respect the player's time.
- Never create pay-to-win mechanics.
- Offer meaningful value.
- Keep the base game enjoyable.
- Make purchases optional.
- Be transparent about pricing and rewards.

Player trust is more valuable than short-term revenue.

---

# Revenue Model

Anu Sabi follows a **Free-to-Play** model.

The core gameplay experience remains free for all players.

Revenue is generated through optional purchases that expand or personalize the experience without affecting competitive fairness.

---

# Free Player Experience

Free players should always have access to:

- Core gameplay
- Daily challenges
- Standard categories
- Progression system
- Badges
- Leaderboards
- Daily rewards
- Achievements

A player should be able to enjoy the game indefinitely without making a purchase.

---

# Premium Experience

Premium purchases may provide:

- Additional phrase packs
- Exclusive categories
- Cosmetic themes
- Premium avatars
- Profile customization
- Seasonal content
- Early access to new content

Premium content should increase variety, not player power.

---

# Cosmetic Purchases

Cosmetics allow players to personalize their experience.

Examples include:

- Themes
- Avatars
- Profile frames
- Badge effects
- Celebration animations
- Alternate UI skins

Cosmetics should never influence gameplay performance.

---

# Content Packs

Additional phrase packs may be offered as optional purchases.

Examples:

- Philippine History
- Famous Quotes
- Regional Languages
- Food & Cuisine
- Movies & Television
- Music Hits
- Science & Technology
- Holiday Specials

Content packs extend replayability while preserving game balance.

---

# Advertising

Advertising should remain optional.

Possible implementations include:

- Rewarded video ads
- Optional bonus rewards
- Event reward multipliers

Advertising should never interrupt gameplay or appear between puzzles.

Interstitial advertisements should be avoided.

---

# Subscription (Future Consideration)

A future premium subscription may include:

- Exclusive monthly content
- Cosmetic rewards
- Early access to phrase packs
- Expanded statistics
- Seasonal themes

Subscriptions should provide convenience and variety rather than competitive advantages.

---

# Limited-Time Content

Seasonal content may include:

- Holiday phrase packs
- Anniversary events
- Cultural celebrations
- Community events

Limited-time content should eventually return or rotate to avoid creating permanent fear of missing out (FOMO).

---

# Pricing Principles

Pricing should be:

- Affordable
- Transparent
- Fair across regions where possible
- Clearly communicated
- Consistent

Bundles should provide clear value without pressuring purchases.

---

# What We Will Never Sell

The following will never be monetized:

- Correct answers
- Higher scores
- Faster progression
- Exclusive competitive advantages
- Permanent gameplay boosts
- Win conditions

Anu Sabi is committed to maintaining fair gameplay for all players.

---

# Player Trust

Maintaining player trust requires:

- Honest pricing
- No hidden fees
- Clear purchase descriptions
- Respectful advertising
- No manipulative spending mechanics

Trust should be treated as a long-term asset.

---

# Future Opportunities

Potential future monetization options include:

- Creator collaboration packs
- Educational bundles
- Charity content packs
- Regional language collections
- Merchandise integration
- Supporter editions

Any future offering should continue to respect the principles established in this document.

---

# Success Metrics

The monetization strategy is successful when:

- Players willingly support the game.
- Purchases feel valuable.
- Free players enjoy the complete core experience.
- Revenue supports continued development.
- Community trust remains high.
