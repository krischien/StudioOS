# 🔥 Daily Engagement & Retention

> *Players should return because they enjoy the game, not because they fear missing out.*

---

# Purpose

This document defines the engagement systems that encourage regular play while respecting the player's time and avoiding manipulative mechanics.

Retention should strengthen enjoyment, not create obligation.

---

# Retention Philosophy

Anu Sabi follows five guiding principles:

- Reward consistency.
- Respect player breaks.
- Celebrate milestones.
- Encourage exploration.
- Avoid excessive fear of missing out (FOMO).

Missing a day should never make players feel punished.

---

# Engagement Systems

The game includes several systems designed to encourage continued play.

## Daily Login Rewards

Players receive a reward for logging in each day.

Possible rewards include:

- Coins
- XP
- Cosmetic items
- Hint tokens
- Content unlock fragments
- Limited-time event rewards

Rewards should gradually increase throughout a reward cycle.

---

## Daily Streaks

A streak tracks consecutive days of activity.

Possible milestone rewards:

- Day 3
- Day 7
- Day 14
- Day 30
- Day 100

Milestones celebrate dedication without creating gameplay advantages.

---

# Flexible Streak Policy

The game should avoid harsh penalties.

Possible implementation:

- One free streak recovery each month.
- Grace period for missed days.
- Optional streak protection rewards.

The goal is to encourage players, not discourage them.

---

# Daily Missions

Players receive a small set of rotating objectives.

Examples:

- Complete 3 rounds.
- Solve 15 phrases.
- Earn 250 coins.
- Finish one category.
- Play for 10 minutes.

Daily missions provide additional reasons to return.

---

# Weekly Challenges

Weekly objectives encourage longer-term engagement.

Examples:

- Complete 50 puzzles.
- Earn 5,000 coins.
- Reach 90% accuracy.
- Unlock a new badge.
- Complete multiple categories.

Weekly rewards should feel meaningful but optional.

---

# Comeback Rewards

Players returning after an extended absence should receive a warm welcome.

Possible rewards:

- Bonus coins
- Extra XP
- Limited-time missions
- Welcome-back gift pack

Returning players should feel appreciated rather than penalized.

---

# Achievement Integration

Retention systems should connect naturally with achievements.

Examples:

- First Login
- 7-Day Streak
- 30-Day Streak
- Mission Master
- Weekend Warrior

Achievements recognize consistency without creating pressure.

---

# Seasonal Engagement

Special events may include:

- Christmas rewards
- Independence Day missions
- Anniversary celebrations
- Community events
- Holiday phrase packs

Seasonal events should introduce fresh experiences while remaining accessible.

---

# Notifications (Future)

Optional notifications may remind players about:

- Daily rewards
- New content packs
- Seasonal events
- Weekly challenges

Notifications should always be:

- Optional
- Relevant
- Respectful
- Easy to disable

---

# Retention Metrics

The following metrics may be monitored to evaluate player engagement:

- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Weekly Active Users (WAU)
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Average Session Length
- Consecutive Login Days
- Mission Completion Rate
- Return Rate
- Player Lifetime Value (LTV)

These metrics should guide improvements rather than dictate manipulative design.

---

# Design Principles

Retention systems should:

- Reward effort.
- Respect player time.
- Avoid unnecessary pressure.
- Support healthy play habits.
- Encourage long-term enjoyment.

---

# Current Features

Current implementation includes:

- Daily login rewards
- Daily streak tracking
- Coin rewards
- Badge progression

---

# Planned Features

Future additions may include:

- Weekly missions
- Monthly objectives
- Seasonal campaigns
- Community events
- Returning player campaigns
- Personalized challenges

These features should build upon the existing retention philosophy.

---

# Success Metrics

The retention system is successful when:

- Players return regularly because they enjoy the experience.
- Streaks feel rewarding rather than stressful.
- Daily missions remain varied and achievable.
- Returning players feel welcomed.
- Long-term engagement grows naturally.
