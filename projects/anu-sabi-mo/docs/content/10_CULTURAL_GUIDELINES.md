# 🌏 Cultural Guidelines

> **Defines the principles for creating culturally respectful, inclusive, and engaging content within Anu Sabi.**

---

# Purpose

Anu Sabi celebrates language, culture, entertainment, and shared knowledge.

These guidelines ensure that all content is created with respect for diverse cultures, backgrounds, and communities while maintaining an enjoyable gameplay experience.

Cultural awareness strengthens the game's accessibility and long-term relevance.

---

# Design Principles

Content should be:

- Respectful
- Inclusive
- Accurate
- Accessible
- Educational where appropriate
- Enjoyable

Players should feel welcomed regardless of their background.

---

# Cultural Representation

The game should represent a wide variety of cultures over time.

Examples include:

- Countries
- Languages
- Traditions
- Festivals
- Foods
- Historical figures
- Literature
- Music
- Sports
- Entertainment

Balanced representation enriches the content library.

---

# Avoiding Stereotypes

Content should never rely on harmful stereotypes.

Avoid:

- Ethnic stereotypes
- National stereotypes
- Religious stereotypes
- Gender stereotypes
- Cultural caricatures

Humor should never come at the expense of a community or identity.

---

# Respectful Language

Use language that is respectful and appropriate for a broad audience.

Avoid:

- Slurs
- Hate speech
- Derogatory expressions
- Offensive nicknames
- Insults targeting protected groups

Respectful communication is a core editorial requirement.

---

# Historical Content

Historical references should strive for factual accuracy.

When including:

- Historical figures
- Historical events
- Important discoveries
- Cultural landmarks

Content should avoid misleading or sensationalized presentations.

---

# Religion

Religion should be treated respectfully.

Acceptable examples include:

- Religious holidays
- Places of worship
- Well-known historical figures
- Religious literature
- Major traditions

Avoid content that mocks, demeans, or misrepresents religious beliefs.

---

# Politics

Political content should be approached with caution.

The game should avoid:

- Partisan messaging
- Campaign slogans
- Political propaganda
- Current political disputes

Historical political references may be included when presented objectively.

---

# Humor

Humor is an important part of Anu Sabi.

Humor should be:

- Clever
- Inclusive
- Family-friendly
- Language-focused
- Situation-based

Humor should never depend on insulting people or cultures.

---

# Pop Culture

Pop culture references are encouraged when they are:

- Widely recognizable
- Appropriate for the target audience
- Legally usable
- Suitable for gameplay

Popularity may change over time, so older references should be reviewed periodically.

---

# Educational Value

Whenever appropriate, content should encourage curiosity.

Examples include:

- Geography
- Science
- Literature
- History
- Languages
- Nature

Educational content should remain engaging rather than instructional.

---

# Age Appropriateness

Content should generally be suitable for a broad audience.

Avoid:

- Graphic violence
- Explicit sexual content
- Profanity
- Illegal drug promotion
- Gambling promotion
- Extreme horror

The goal is to maintain an experience appropriate for players of varying ages.

---

# Regional Adaptation

Some content may require regional adjustments.

Examples:

- Local celebrities
- Television programs
- Sports leagues
- Idioms
- Slang
- Educational topics

Regional adaptations should preserve the intended gameplay experience.

---

# Accessibility

Content should remain understandable to the intended audience.

When appropriate:

- Prefer commonly recognized references.
- Avoid unnecessary complexity.
- Minimize highly obscure cultural references unless clearly categorized.

Accessibility improves fairness.

---

# Community Feedback

Player feedback should help improve cultural representation.

Feedback may identify:

- Outdated terminology
- Inaccurate references
- Regional preferences
- Missing representation
- Cultural sensitivity concerns

Constructive feedback should be incorporated into future revisions when appropriate.

---

# AI-Assisted Content

AI-generated content should be reviewed for:

- Cultural accuracy
- Respectful wording
- Regional appropriateness
- Bias
- Stereotypes
- Offensive implications

Human review remains essential.

---

# Rambler Engine Considerations

The Rambler Engine should generate culturally appropriate content by:

- Respecting category rules
- Avoiding offensive combinations
- Favoring broadly recognizable references
- Applying regional filters where appropriate

Generated content should always follow these cultural guidelines.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Cultural understanding evolves over time.

The content library should be reviewed periodically to:

- Improve representation
- Remove outdated material
- Update terminology
- Reflect evolving language usage
- Expand global coverage

Continuous review keeps the game welcoming and relevant.
