# 🤖 AI-Assisted Content

> **Defines how artificial intelligence assists with creating, reviewing, improving, and maintaining content within Anu Sabi.**

---

# Purpose

Artificial intelligence is a productivity tool that helps expand and improve the Anu Sabi content library.

AI should increase efficiency while maintaining the editorial, gameplay, and cultural standards established throughout this documentation.

Human review remains the final authority for all released content.

---

# Design Goals

AI integration should be:

- Helpful
- Consistent
- Explainable
- Scalable
- Reviewable
- Safe

The objective is to assist content creators, not replace them.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI may assist with:

- Drafting phrases
- Suggesting categories
- Estimating difficulty
- Generating metadata
- Grammar checking
- Spell checking
- Duplicate detection
- Translation assistance
- Editorial consistency
- Analytics recommendations

AI should accelerate repetitive tasks while preserving human oversight.

---

# AI Workflow

```
Prompt

↓

AI Generation

↓

Metadata Suggestion

↓

Validation

↓

Editorial Review

↓

Gameplay Review

↓

Approval

↓

Release
```

AI-generated content follows the same workflow as manually created content.

---

# Phrase Generation

AI may generate:

- New phrase candidates
- Educational phrases
- Entertainment references
- Category suggestions
- Event-themed content
- Seasonal content

Generated phrases are considered drafts until approved.

---

# Metadata Assistance

AI may recommend metadata including:

- Category
- Difficulty
- Language
- Region
- Tags
- Source
- Keywords

Editors should verify all suggested metadata.

---

# Editorial Assistance

AI may identify:

- Grammar issues
- Spelling mistakes
- Formatting inconsistencies
- Missing metadata
- Duplicate phrases
- Naming inconsistencies

Editorial suggestions should always be reviewed before acceptance.

---

# Difficulty Estimation

AI may estimate difficulty using factors such as:

- Familiarity
- Vocabulary complexity
- Phrase length
- Cultural recognition
- Solve complexity

Difficulty estimates are recommendations, not final decisions.

---

# Localization Assistance

AI may assist with:

- Translation
- Grammar correction
- Regional wording
- Localization consistency
- Metadata translation

Human reviewers should confirm cultural accuracy and gameplay suitability.

---

# Rambler Engine Support

The Rambler Engine may use AI to:

- Generate candidate phrases
- Expand category libraries
- Recommend difficulty
- Detect duplicates
- Improve metadata
- Suggest phrase variations

AI-enhanced generation remains subject to the same validation pipeline as all other content.

---

# Analytics Assistance

AI may analyze gameplay data to recommend:

- Difficulty adjustments
- Category balancing
- Phrase retirement
- Hint improvements
- Content expansion opportunities

Analytics should guide decision-making rather than automate it.

---

# Quality Assurance

AI-generated content should be evaluated using the same standards as manually authored content.

Review should verify:

- Editorial quality
- Gameplay fairness
- Cultural appropriateness
- Metadata accuracy
- Difficulty consistency

No content should bypass review because it was AI-generated.

---

# Safety Guidelines

AI should not generate content that includes:

- Hate speech
- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Explicit sexual content
- Graphic violence
- Illegal activities
- Dangerous misinformation
- Offensive stereotypes

Safety requirements apply equally to manual and AI-generated content.

---

# Human Oversight

Editors remain responsible for:

- Final approval
- Editorial decisions
- Gameplay quality
- Cultural review
- Content releases

AI provides recommendations rather than authoritative decisions.

---

# Continuous Improvement

AI-assisted workflows should improve over time using:

- Editorial feedback
- Gameplay analytics
- Player feedback
- Error reports
- Review outcomes

Continuous learning improves recommendation quality without replacing human judgment.

---

# Future Capabilities

Future AI features may include:

- Intelligent phrase expansion
- Adaptive content recommendations
- Automated metadata generation
- Editorial linting
- Translation quality scoring
- Semantic duplicate detection
- Personalized content suggestions

Future enhancements should remain compatible with the existing content governance framework.
