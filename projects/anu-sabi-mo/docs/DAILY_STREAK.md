# Daily Streak & Player Retention System

**Document Version:** 3.0
**Status:** Living Document
**Project:** Anu Sabi
**Owner:** Kristian
**Last Updated:** February 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the systems that encourage players to return to Anu Sabi regularly.

The goal is to build long-term engagement through meaningful rewards, evolving content, and positive habits rather than fear of missing out (FOMO).
 
Retention should feel exciting, not obligatory.

---

# 2. Retention Philosophy

Players should return because:

* There is something new to discover.
* Their progress feels meaningful.
* Their friends are playing.
* They enjoy the experience.

Players should never feel punished for taking a break.

---

# 3. Design Goals

The retention system should:

* Encourage daily play.
* Respect player schedules.
* Reward consistency.
* Avoid excessive pressure.
* Support long-term engagement.
* Integrate naturally with the game's social features.

---

# 5. Daily Login Rewards

Players receive a reward each day they log in.

Example Weekly Cycle

| Day | Reward         |
| --- | -------------- |
| 1   | Coins          |
| 2   | XP Boost       |
| 3   | Avatar Sticker |
| 4   | Coins          |
| 5   | Content Token  |
| 6   | Profile Frame  |
| 7   | Mystery Chest  |

The cycle repeats with increasing milestone rewards.

---

# 6. Forgiving Streak System

Anu Sabi avoids harsh streak resets.

If a player misses a day:

* A short grace period may apply.
* Players may use earned "Streak Protect" items (future).
* Milestones remain achievable.

This reduces frustration while still encouraging consistency.

---

# 7. Daily Missions

Examples include:

* Play 2 Matches
* Vote in Every Round
* Win 1 Match
* Try a New Content Pack
* Invite a Friend
* React to 5 Answers
* Finish a Match Without Leaving

Daily missions should require approximately 10–20 minutes.

---

# 8. Weekly Challenges

Longer objectives encourage sustained play.

Examples:

* Play 25 Matches
* Earn 1,500 XP
* Complete 5 Daily Missions
* Unlock a Cosmetic
* Receive 100 Votes

Weekly challenges offer larger rewards.

---

# 9. Monthly Milestones

Monthly goals may include:

* Play 100 Matches
* Collect Every Daily Reward
* Complete Every Weekly Challenge
* Reach a New Player Level

Rewards may include exclusive cosmetics and titles.

---

# 10. Seasonal Retention

Seasonal events provide:

* Limited-time missions
* Holiday content
* Exclusive avatars
* Event cosmetics
* Temporary achievements

Seasonal content should refresh the game without replacing the core experience.

---

# 11. Social Retention

Players are more likely to return when their friends are active.

Examples:

* Friend activity feed
* Rematch invitations
* Team challenges
* Group milestones
* Community events

Social interaction is one of the strongest retention drivers.

---

# 12. Progress Visibility

Players should always see meaningful progress.

Examples:

* XP Bar
* Mission Progress
* Collection Completion
* Achievement Tracker
* Streak Counter
* Event Progress

Visible progress reinforces motivation.

---

# 13. Collection Goals

Collections encourage long-term engagement.

Examples:

* Complete all avatars.
* Unlock every title.
* Collect every content pack.
* Earn seasonal cosmetics.
* Finish achievement sets.

---

# 14. Surprise Rewards

Occasional unexpected rewards create excitement.

Examples:

* Mystery Chest
* Random Cosmetic
* Double XP Day
* Community Celebration Reward
* Anniversary Gift

Surprises should feel generous rather than manipulative.

---

# 15. Returning Player Experience

When a player returns after an absence:

* Welcome them back warmly.
* Summarize what is new.
* Offer a catch-up reward.
* Highlight active events.
* Suggest an easy mission to re-engage.

Avoid making returning players feel left behind.

---

# 16. Notification Strategy

Notifications should be helpful rather than intrusive.

Examples:

* Daily reward available
* Friend invited you
* Event ending soon
* New content pack released

Players should be able to customize notification preferences.

---

# 17. Anti-FOMO Principles

Anu Sabi intentionally avoids excessive fear of missing out.

Guidelines:

* Seasonal items may return.
* Missed rewards should not permanently block progression.
* Catch-up mechanics should exist where appropriate.
* Players should not feel punished for taking breaks.

---

# 18. Decision Rationale

### Why a forgiving streak system?

Players have busy lives. A single missed day should not erase weeks of progress.

### Why focus on social retention?

People return to experiences they share with friends. Encouraging group play increases long-term engagement naturally.

### Why include surprise rewards?

Unexpected positive moments increase excitement and create memorable experiences without requiring constant high-value rewards.

---

# 19. Success Metrics

The retention system succeeds when:

* Daily Active Users (DAU) increase.
* 7-day retention improves.
* 30-day retention improves.
* Players complete daily missions.
* Returning players continue playing after re-engaging.
* Community participation grows.
