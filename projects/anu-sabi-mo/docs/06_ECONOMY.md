# 💰 Economy & Progression

> *Every game session should leave the player feeling they made meaningful progress.*

---

# Purpose

The economy system exists to reward player effort, encourage continued play, and provide long-term goals without creating unnecessary grinding or pay-to-win mechanics.

Progression should feel satisfying, fair, and transparent.

---

# Economy Philosophy

The Anu Sabi economy follows four guiding principles:

- Reward consistent play.
- Celebrate player achievements.
- Encourage exploration of new content.
- Never punish players for taking breaks.

Players should feel that every completed session contributes to their overall progress.

---

# Core Progression Systems

Anu Sabi uses several interconnected progression systems.

## Coins

Coins are the primary in-game currency.

Players earn coins by:

- Completing rounds
- Correct answers
- Daily rewards
- Achievements
- Streak bonuses
- Special events

Coins are used to:

- Purchase hint packs
- Unlock premium categories (if offered)
- Unlock cosmetic items
- Purchase future content packs

---

## Experience (XP)

XP measures player growth.

XP is earned through:

- Completing rounds
- Consecutive correct answers
- Finishing challenges
- Completing achievements

XP cannot be purchased.

XP determines:

- Player Level
- Rank progression
- Unlock milestones

---

## Levels

Levels represent long-term player progression.

Benefits may include:

- New badges
- New content
- Achievement milestones
- Cosmetic unlocks

Levels should require increasing amounts of XP over time while remaining achievable.

---

## Ranks

Ranks communicate player experience.

Example progression:

- Beginner
- Novice
- Explorer
- Wordsmith
- Fluent
- Master
- Legend

Ranks are symbolic achievements rather than competitive advantages.

---

## Badges

Badges celebrate accomplishments.

Examples:

- First Correct Answer
- Perfect Round
- Seven-Day Streak
- Category Master
- Speed Solver

Badges provide recognition rather than gameplay advantages.

---

# Rewards

Rewards should be granted for:

- Playing
- Winning
- Improving
- Returning
- Exploring
- Completing collections

Even unsuccessful rounds should provide small rewards to encourage persistence.

---

# Reward Sources

Players may earn rewards through:

- Gameplay
- Daily Login
- Daily Challenges
- Weekly Challenges
- Achievement Completion
- Special Events
- Seasonal Content

---

# Unlock System

Unlockable content may include:

- Categories
- Phrase Packs
- Themes
- Avatars
- Profile Decorations
- Titles

Unlocks should represent progress and personalize the player experience.

---

# Daily Rewards

Daily engagement is encouraged through:

- Login rewards
- Daily streak bonuses
- Daily challenges
- Rotating objectives

Missing a day should reduce pressure while still rewarding consistency.

Detailed mechanics are defined in **09_DAILY_STREAK.md**.

---

# Achievement Progression

Achievements provide optional goals.

Categories include:

- Gameplay
- Accuracy
- Speed
- Collection
- Daily Participation
- Social Features

Achievements should reward dedication rather than luck.

---

# Reward Balance

Rewards should scale based on:

- Difficulty
- Accuracy
- Completion
- Speed
- Challenge type

Harder content should provide proportionally greater rewards.

---

# Economy Balance Principles

The economy should always:

- Avoid excessive grinding.
- Prevent inflation.
- Reward skill and consistency.
- Encourage experimentation.
- Support long-term engagement.

The system should remain sustainable as new content is introduced.

---

# Future Expansion

Future progression systems may include:

- Seasonal Passes
- Prestige Levels
- Limited-Time Rewards
- Collection Albums
- Community Milestones
- Event Currency

These systems should complement, not replace, the core progression loop.

---

# Success Metrics

The economy is considered successful when:

- Players feel rewarded after every session.
- Progress is steady and understandable.
- Unlocks remain meaningful.
- Long-term goals encourage continued play.
- No feature creates a pay-to-win advantage.
