# Monetization Strategy

**Document Version:** 3.0
**Status:** Living Document
**Project:** Anu Sabi
**Owner:** Kristian

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the monetization philosophy and systems for Anu Sabi.

The objective is to create a sustainable business model while preserving a fun, fair, and player-friendly experience.

Revenue should enable the continued development of the game without creating unfair advantages or frustrating players.

---

# 2. Monetization Philosophy

Our guiding principles are:

* Fun comes before monetization.
* Players should never feel forced to spend money.
* Purchases should enhance personalization and content, not competitive strength.
* Free players should enjoy the complete core game.
* Paying players should support the game's growth, not gain gameplay power.

---

# 3. Design Goals

The monetization system should:

* Be transparent.
* Respect the player's time.
* Avoid manipulative tactics.
* Reward long-term engagement.
* Encourage optional purchases.
* Support years of content updates.

---

# 4. Revenue Model

Primary revenue sources:

* Cosmetic purchases
* Premium content packs
* Seasonal Pass (future)
* Optional ad rewards
* Limited-time event bundles

Future opportunities:

* Brand collaborations
* Creator partnerships
* Merchandise
* Tournament sponsorships

---

# 5. What Players Can Buy

Examples include:

* Avatar bundles
* Profile frames
* Animated profile backgrounds
* Emoji packs
* Reaction packs
* Victory animations
* Name effects
* Seasonal cosmetics
* Theme packs
* Premium content packs

These purchases do not affect gameplay balance.

---

# 6. Content Packs

Premium content packs may include:

* New conversation themes
* Exclusive prompt collections
* Holiday events
* Pop culture-inspired themes (where licensing permits)
* Regional language packs

Most content packs should also have a gameplay unlock path whenever practical.

---

# 7. Cosmetic Philosophy

Cosmetics are the primary monetization pillar.

Examples:

* Character avatars
* Lobby decorations
* Animated borders
* Card designs
* Chat reactions
* Profile themes
* Celebration effects

Cosmetics allow self-expression without creating competitive advantages.

---

# 8. Advertising

Advertising should remain optional whenever possible.

Preferred ad placements:

* Rewarded videos
* Daily reward bonuses
* Optional coin multipliers
* Event bonuses

Avoid:

* Forced ads after every match
* Interruptive pop-ups
* Ads during gameplay
* Excessive ad frequency

---

# 9. Rewarded Ads

Examples:

Watch one ad to receive:

* Extra coins
* Additional XP
* Daily reward multiplier
* Event token
* Cosmetic preview unlock

Participation should always be voluntary.

---

# 10. Premium Membership (Future)

Potential benefits:

* Exclusive cosmetics
* Monthly rewards
* Premium profile customization
* Early access to content packs
* Bonus seasonal rewards

Premium membership should never provide gameplay advantages.

---

# 11. Seasonal Pass (Future)

A seasonal pass may include:

Free Track

* Coins
* XP
* Basic cosmetics

Premium Track

* Exclusive cosmetics
* Premium avatars
* Animated rewards
* Seasonal titles
* Unique profile decorations

---

# 12. Limited-Time Events

Events may introduce:

* Exclusive cosmetic collections
* Holiday avatars
* Event-specific reactions
* Temporary profile themes
* Special titles

Missed items may return in future events to reduce fear of missing out (FOMO).

---

# 13. Bundles

Bundles may combine:

* Cosmetic sets
* Content packs
* Seasonal themes
* Event rewards

Bundles should offer clear value without overwhelming players.

---

# 14. Currency Model

Current

* Coins (earned)

Future

* Premium Currency (purchased)
* Event Tokens (temporary)

The number of currencies should remain minimal to reduce complexity.

---

# 15. Ethical Monetization

Anu Sabi commits to avoiding:

* Pay-to-win mechanics
* Energy systems that block gameplay
* Loot boxes with undisclosed odds
* Forced spending to progress
* Excessive FOMO
* Artificial difficulty spikes designed to encourage purchases

Player trust is a long-term asset.

---

# 16. Player-Friendly Practices

We aim to:

* Clearly display prices.
* Clearly explain purchases.
* Offer generous free rewards.
* Make refunds straightforward where platform policies allow.
* Avoid misleading UI.

---

# 17. Regional Pricing

Where supported by app stores, pricing should consider local purchasing power to improve accessibility across different regions.

---

# 18. Future Opportunities

Potential future monetization avenues:

* Community creator marketplace

---

# 19. Success Metrics

The monetization strategy succeeds when:

* Players continue playing regardless of spending.
* Purchases are viewed positively.
* Retention remains high.
* Cosmetic adoption increases.
* Revenue supports ongoing development.
* Community sentiment remains positive.

---

# 20. Risks

Potential risks include:

* Over-monetization
* Cosmetic saturation
* Reward inflation
* Player perception of unfairness
* Content fragmentation

These risks should be reviewed regularly.

---

# 21. Decision Rationale

### Why cosmetics first?

Cosmetics encourage self-expression without affecting fairness.

### Why optional ads?

Players appreciate having a choice. Optional rewards create goodwill while generating revenue.

### Why avoid pay-to-win?

The heart of Anu Sabi is creativity and social interaction. Competitive advantages purchased with money would undermine that foundation.
