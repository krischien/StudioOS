# 🌟 Product Vision

> *To create the definitive Filipino party word game that brings people together through language, culture, and shared moments of laughter.*

---

# Why Anu Sabi Exists

Language is one of the strongest expressions of culture.

Filipino conversations are filled with expressions, slang, idioms, jokes, and sayings that instantly create connection between people.

Yet very few games celebrate this everyday part of Filipino life.

Anu Sabi exists to transform those familiar words and phrases into an experience that is fun, competitive, educational, and highly replayable.

Rather than teaching language in a classroom setting, the game allows players to experience Filipino culture through play.

---

# Our Mission

Our mission is to create a game that:

- Celebrates Filipino language and identity.
- Encourages social interaction.
- Rewards curiosity and recognition.
- Remains enjoyable after hundreds of play sessions.
- Continues growing through carefully curated content.

---

# Our Long-Term Vision

We envision Anu Sabi becoming:

- The most recognizable Filipino word party game.
- A platform that continuously expands with new content.
- A game enjoyed by families, students, friends, and overseas Filipinos.
- A showcase of Filipino creativity in game development.
- The flagship project of StudioOS.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every feature should answer one question:

> **Does this make the game more fun, more replayable, or more meaningful?**

If the answer is no, the feature should be reconsidered.

The goal is not to create the biggest game.

The goal is to create the most enjoyable one.

---

# Core Design Principles

## 1. Fun Comes First

Gameplay should always be enjoyable before it is educational.

Players return because they are entertained, not because they feel obligated to learn.

---

## 2. Celebrate Filipino Culture

The game should proudly highlight Filipino language, humor, traditions, and everyday expressions without relying on stereotypes.

Content should feel authentic and relatable.

---

## 3. Easy to Learn, Difficult to Master

New players should understand the rules within minutes.

Experienced players should continue finding new challenges through richer content and increasing complexity.

---

## 4. Replayability Over Complexity

Rather than constantly introducing new mechanics, Anu Sabi should grow through:

- Better content
- Smarter progression
- Seasonal updates
- New phrase packs
- Fresh challenges

Replayability should come from variety, not unnecessary complexity.

---

## 5. Respect the Player's Time

Most game sessions should last only a few minutes.

Players should always feel that they made meaningful progress, even during short play sessions.

---

## 6. Build for Longevity

Every major system should be designed so that it can evolve over time without requiring large-scale rewrites.

Scalable architecture and maintainable content are essential to the project's success.

---

# Product Pillars

Everything in Anu Sabi should support at least one of these pillars.

## 🇵🇭 Culture

Celebrate Filipino identity through familiar language and shared experiences.

---

## 🎮 Fun

Every session should create moments of excitement, laughter, and friendly competition.

---

## 🔁 Replayability

Players should always have a reason to come back.

---

## 🏆 Progression

Coins, badges, streaks, unlocks, and achievements should create a satisfying sense of growth.

---

## 📚 Content

The game should continue evolving through carefully curated phrase packs rather than constant gameplay changes.

---

# What Anu Sabi Is Not

To remain focused, the project intentionally avoids becoming:

- A traditional trivia game.
- A classroom learning application.
- A generic word puzzle clone.
- A feature-heavy platform with unnecessary complexity.

The objective is clarity of purpose, not feature accumulation.

---

# Success Looks Like...

A successful Anu Sabi is one where:

- Players smile and laugh together.
- Families play multiple rounds in one sitting.
- Friends challenge each other repeatedly.
- New content keeps the experience fresh.
- Filipino culture is celebrated with authenticity and pride.

---

# Future Vision

In the years ahead, Anu Sabi may expand through:

- New themed phrase packs.
- Seasonal events.
- Community-inspired content.
- AI-assisted content creation tools.
- Cross-platform availability.
- Additional languages while preserving its Filipino-first identity.

These possibilities should always support the core vision rather than distract from it.

---

# Vision Statement

> **Anu Sabi exists to celebrate Filipino language through joyful play, creating meaningful shared experiences that are easy to enjoy, rewarding to master, and built to grow for years to come.**

---

# Decision Filter

When evaluating future ideas, ask the following questions:

- Does this make the game more fun?
- Does it improve replayability?
- Does it celebrate Filipino culture?
- Does it respect the player's time?
- Does it fit the long-term vision?
- Can it be maintained as the game grows?

If the answer to most of these questions is **yes**, the idea likely aligns with the product vision.
