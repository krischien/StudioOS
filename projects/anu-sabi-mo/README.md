# Anu Sabi Mo

A fast-paced Filipino phonetic word puzzle game where players decode gibberish phrases by saying them out loud.

The challenge isn't what you read...

It's what you hear.

---

## Overview

Anu Sabi Mo is an offline-first Android word game inspired by the social fun of saying nonsense words aloud until they suddenly reveal a familiar Filipino word or phrase.

Players read gibberish, pronounce it aloud, and guess the hidden phrase.

Simple to learn.
Hard to master.
Perfect for quick sessions with friends and family.
