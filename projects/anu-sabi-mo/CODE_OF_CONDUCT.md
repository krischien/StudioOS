# 📜 Code of Conduct

Anu Sabi aims to foster a respectful, collaborative, and welcoming development environment.

---

# Our Standards

We encourage:

- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback
- Curiosity and continuous learning
- Professional collaboration
- Inclusive discussions

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Trolling
- Intentionally disruptive behavior

---

# Resolving Issues

If disagreements arise:

1. Focus on the technical or product issue.
2. Assume good intent.
3. Document decisions when appropriate.
4. Seek consensus whenever possible.

---

# AI Usage

AI-generated code, documentation, or content should always be reviewed by a human before becoming part of the project.

---

# Commitment

By participating in the project, contributors agree to uphold these standards.
