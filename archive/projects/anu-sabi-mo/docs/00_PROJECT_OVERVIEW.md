# 🇵🇭 Anu Sabi
## Project Overview

> *A modern Filipino party word game built around language, culture, laughter, and replayability.*

---

# Executive Summary

Anu Sabi is a mobile-first casual party game where players race against time to identify incomplete Filipino words, phrases, idioms, expressions, and cultural references.

The game is designed around short, highly replayable sessions that encourage players to think quickly, recognize familiar language patterns, and enjoy shared cultural experiences.

Unlike traditional quiz games, Anu Sabi emphasizes fun over memorization. Players are rewarded for recognizing everyday language, popular sayings, and uniquely Filipino expressions rather than obscure trivia.

The long-term vision extends beyond a single game. Anu Sabi is being built as a scalable content platform capable of supporting thousands of phrases, themed content packs, seasonal events, and AI-assisted content creation while maintaining a carefully curated player experience.

---

# Elevator Pitch

**Anu Sabi** is a fast-paced Filipino word guessing game where players complete missing words and phrases before time runs out. With hundreds of culturally relevant challenges, rewarding progression systems, and expanding content packs, the game aims to become the definitive Filipino party game for mobile devices.

# Design Pillars

Every feature added to Anu Sabi should support one or more of these pillars.

## 1. Easy to Learn

Players should understand the basic mechanics within minutes.

---

## 2. Highly Replayable

The game should remain enjoyable after hundreds of rounds through varied content, progression, and challenge.

---

## 3. Celebrate Filipino Culture

Language, expressions, humor, and shared experiences are the heart of the game.

---

## 4. Reward Progress

Players should consistently feel a sense of achievement through badges, coins, streaks, and content unlocks.

---

## 5. Built to Grow

The architecture should support new content, features, and systems without requiring major redesigns.
